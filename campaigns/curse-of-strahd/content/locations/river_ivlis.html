Barovia Region

When the characters come within sight of this river for the first time, read: I This river flows as clear as a blue winter sky through the valley. The river is roughly 50 feet wide, with a depth ranging from 5 to 10 feet. Arching stone bridges span the river at two points, one near the village of Barovia (area E) and the other near [[tser_falls|Tser Falls]] (area H).
