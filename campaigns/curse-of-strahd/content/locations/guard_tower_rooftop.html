Ten-foot-tall, gold-plated statues stand atop the battle­ ments, facing outward. Each one depicts a female hu­ man knight holding a lance. The cold wind stirs the snow, under which you see human skeletons clad in rusty mail. The roof is 40 feet high and 540 feet above the misty val­ ley below.
