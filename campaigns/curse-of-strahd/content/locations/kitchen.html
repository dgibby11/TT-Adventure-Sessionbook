A horrible odor of decay fills this steaming hot room. A huge pot bubbles over a blazing fire pit in the center of the room, its green, muddy contents churning. The far wall is lined with pegs, hanging from which are numer­ ous large cooking implements-some of which could easily double as implements of torture. l If a character looks into the pot, three human zombies rise up from the bubbling depths and attack. The zom­ bies are slowly being boiled to death, and each has only 13 hit points remaining. If [[cyrus_belview|Cyrus Belview]] (see area K62) is present when the zombies attack, he grabs a heavy club and tries to beat them back into the pot. Cyrus ex­ plains that he just isn't the cook he used to be, and his meals tend to get out of hand these days.
