[[village_of_krezk|Village of Krezk]] / Abbey

Scarecrows line the abbey walls, looking outward. They wear tattered chain shirts and carry spears with rusty heads. The courtyard below is blanketed with fog. The scarecrows are lashed to wooden stands. Though fearsome from a distance, they have no life to them. CHAPTER 8 I THE VILLAGE OF KREZK It's a 15-foot drop from the top of the wall to the court­ yard. Any creature that falls over the southwest wall tumbles 400 feet down the cliffside.
