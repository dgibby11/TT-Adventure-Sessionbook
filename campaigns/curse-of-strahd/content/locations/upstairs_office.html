[[village_of_krezk|Village of Krezk]] / Abbey

A wooden counter shaped like an L stands at the front of this spacious office. All the other furniture has rotted away, leaving heaps of moldy wood and faded cloth. The wood of the counter is old, soft, and easily broken. Nothing of value remains here. If the characters haven't already cleared out the madhouse (area S15), they can hear the whoops, laughter, and screams of the mongrel­ folk below. The clamor continues as they explore areas S21-S24 to the east.
