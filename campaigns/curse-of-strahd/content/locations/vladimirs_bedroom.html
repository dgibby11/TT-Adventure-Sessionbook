[[argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]]

Light enters this circular room through five cracked win­ dows. The light falls on a large, dust-covered bed in the center of the room, its posts topped with wood-carved dragons. Two large animals flank the double doors. One is a brown bear standing on its hind legs, its claws out­ stretched. The other is a dire wolf, its face frozen in an evil snarl. Near the wolf lies an empty wooden chest. This room once served as a bedchamber for Sir Vladi­ mir Horngaard and Sir Godfrey Gwilym. The bear and the dire wolf are stuffed and harmless. Looters ran­ sacked the chest long ago, leaving nothing of value.
