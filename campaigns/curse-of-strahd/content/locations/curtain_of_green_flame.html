[[tsolenka_pass|Tsolenka Pass]]

A curtain of green flame fills the eastern archway of the gatehouse. Any creature that enters the curtain for the first time on a turn or starts its turn in the green flame takes 33 (6d10) fire damage. A successful casting of dispel magic (DC 16) sup­ presses the curtain for 1 minute. The curtain is also suppressed within an antimagic field.
