[[castle_ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]]

This thirty-foot-square room is a shambles. Scattered fur­ niture lies in heaps near the walls. Broken bones lie scat­ tered amid crumpled and crushed plate armor. Shields and swords jut from the walls as if driven into them by some tremendous force. Two doors stand opposite one another in the center of the north wall and the south wall. A dark archway leads out through the east wall. After [[strahd_von_zarovich|Strahd]] was transformed into a vampire, several of the castle guards retreated to this room, but Strahd caught them and slaughtered them in a brutal show of violence. Removing one of the shields or swords from the wall requires a successful DC 10 Strength check. None of the items found here are valuable.
