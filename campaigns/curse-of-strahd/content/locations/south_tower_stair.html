[[castle_ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]]

Fluttering torches in iron sconces illuminate this spiral staircase. A chill wind rushes down the circling stairway, seeming to kill the very heat of the torches. These stairs start at area K73 and. go up through areas K61, K9, K30, and K35 before ending at area K47.
