The Mystery

How can the party permanently destroy [[strahd_von_zarovich|Strahd von Zarovich]] and free Barovia from his curse?

Answer

Strahd can be defeated in combat, but he won't stay dead — he returns to his coffin and regenerates. To permanently destroy him, the party needs all three of the following:

  • The [[sunsword|Sunsword]] — to deal radiant damage and keep him from regenerating
  • The [[holy_symbol_of_ravenkind|Holy Symbol of Ravenkind]] — to turn/paralyze him during the final fight
  • Drive a wooden stake through his heart while he sleeps in his coffin (area [[strahds_tomb|K86]])

The Tarokka Reading

[[madam_eva|Madam Eva]]'s Tarokka reading determines where the three treasures are hidden. Run the reading before the session begins — the card draws are random, placing items in locations keyed by card. See [[fortunes_of_ravenloft_mystery|Fortunes of Ravenloft]].

Final Encounter

The location of the final showdown is also determined by the Tarokka reading. Strahd fights to win; he uses all legendary actions and lair actions when in [[castle_ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]].
