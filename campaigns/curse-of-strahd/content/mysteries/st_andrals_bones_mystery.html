The Mystery

The bones of St. Andral have been stolen from [[st_andral_feast|St. Andral's church]] in [[town_of_vallaki|Vallaki]]. Without them, the church loses its protection against undead — and Strahd's forces can enter.

The Truth

[[izek_strazni|Izek Strazni]] stole the bones on Lady Wachter's orders and gave them to the coffin maker Henrik van der Voort (area [[coffin_makers_shop|N6]]). Henrik is hiding them in a secret room full of vampire spawn.

Timing

If the party doesn't recover the bones before the Feast of St. Andral (the day it's meant to be celebrated), vampire spawn attack the church during the feast. Father Lucian and many townspeople die. The party has ~5 days after arriving in Vallaki to resolve this.
