The Mystery

What happened to [[argynvostholt|Argynvost]]'s skull? Can the phantom knights of [[argynvostholt|Argynvostholt]] ever find peace?

The Truth

After Strahd slew the silver dragon Argynvost, he had the dragon's skull transported to [[castle_ravenloft|Castle Ravenloft]] as a trophy. Without his skull, Argynvost's spirit cannot rest, and his knights — revenants under [[vladimir_horngaard|Vladimir Horngaard]] — remain bound to undeath in a rage that has twisted into pointless hatred.

Resolution

If the party retrieves Argynvost's skull from the castle and returns it to the beacon tower at Argynvostholt, the dragon's spirit is finally able to manifest. The beacon blazes with silver light — a symbol of hope that temporarily weakens Strahd. All the revenants are released from their torment and pass on.

DM Notes

Vladimir Horngaard won't believe anyone can help him. He must be either persuaded or avoided. The skull is in area K67 (Hall of Bones) in Castle Ravenloft.
