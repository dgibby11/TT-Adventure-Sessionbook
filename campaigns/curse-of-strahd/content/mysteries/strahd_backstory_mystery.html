The Mystery

How did [[strahd_von_zarovich|Strahd]] become a vampire? What are the Dark Powers?

The Truth

Strahd von Zarovich was a great conqueror who grew old and bitter watching his younger brother Sergei win glory, love, and youth that Strahd felt was his by right. He made a pact with the Dark Powers — entities that dwell in the [[amber_sarcophagi|Amber Sarcophagi]] — trading his soul for eternal youth and power. On Sergei's wedding day, Strahd murdered him, was bitten by a vampire, and drank Tatyana's blood. The Dark Powers accepted his dark deed and transformed him into the first vampire. Barovia then became his prison domain — a demiplane of dread.

How Players Learn This

The full account is written in the [[tome_of_strahd|Tome of Strahd]], which the party must find via the Tarokka reading. Excerpts are also in Appendix F (handout).
