The Tarokka Reading

Before the adventure begins, [[madam_eva|Madam Eva]] reads the [[tarokka_deck|Tarokka cards]] for the party. The reading determines:

  • Where the [[tome_of_strahd|Tome of Strahd]] is hidden
  • Where the [[holy_symbol_of_ravenkind|Holy Symbol of Ravenkind]] is hidden
  • Where the [[sunsword|Sunsword]] is hidden
  • Who the party's powerful ally is
  • Where [[strahd_von_zarovich|Strahd]] awaits his final battle

How to Run the Reading

Draw five cards from the Tarokka deck before Session 1 (or use the random card tables in Appendix E). Each card maps to a specific location in Barovia. Record the results and place the items/NPCs there at the start of the campaign.
