The Mystery

Why is [[strahd_von_zarovich|Strahd]] so obsessed with [[ireena_kolyana|Ireena Kolyana]]?

The Truth

Ireena's soul is a reincarnation of Tatyana Federovna, a woman Strahd fell in love with centuries ago. When Strahd murdered his brother [[sergei_von_zarovich|Sergei]] on their wedding day to claim Tatyana for himself, she fled and threw herself from the castle walls. Her soul has been reincarnating in Barovia ever since, and Strahd has been hunting her across lifetimes.

Resolution

If Ireena reaches the blessed pool at [[pool_and_shrine|the shrine in Krezk]], the spirit of Sergei appears and takes Tatyana's soul to the afterlife, freeing her from the cycle. Strahd cannot follow. This removes Ireena from Strahd's reach permanently but ends her as a companion.
