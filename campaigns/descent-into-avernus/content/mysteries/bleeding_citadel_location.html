Where Is the Bleeding Citadel?

The Mystery

The [[sword_of_zariel|Sword of Zariel]] is hidden in the Bleeding Citadel — a fragment of the divine realm of Mount Celestia, trapped in hell. But where in Avernus is it?

The Answer

Only [[lulu|Lulu]] knows — and her memories of the location were suppressed. The party must help Lulu recover her memories by exploring Avernus and experiencing key locations that trigger her recall. Eventually she remembers the Citadel's location and can lead the party there.

DM Notes

This is the pacing mechanism for Ch.3. The hexcrawl serves to restore Lulu's memories. Don't rush it — each memory unlock should feel like a revelation.
