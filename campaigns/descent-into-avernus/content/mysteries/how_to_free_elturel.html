Main Quest

The Mystery

Elturel has been dragged into Avernus, chained above the River Styx. How can the city be freed?

Answer — Two Paths

Path 1 — Break the Chains: Find a way to sever the infernal chains binding Elturel to Avernus. This requires dealing with [[archduke_bel|Bel]], [[tiamat|Tiamat]], or other Avernus power players to gain leverage or a ritual.

Path 2 — Redeem Zariel: Retrieve the [[sword_of_zariel|Sword of Zariel]] from the [[bleeding_citadel|Bleeding Citadel]] and use it to inspire [[zariel|Zariel]] to renounce her deal with Asmodeus. If Zariel redeems herself, she breaks the contract and Elturel is freed.

DM Notes

Both paths are valid. The adventure supports multiple endings. Track what leverage the party is building.
