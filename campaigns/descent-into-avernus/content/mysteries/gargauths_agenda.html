What Does Gargauth Want?

The Mystery

The [[shield_of_the_hidden_lord|Shield of the Hidden Lord]] hides a devil called [[gargauth|Gargauth]]. What does he want?

The Truth

Gargauth is a devil who once sought godhood. He is trapped in the shield, which was placed in [[v36_vanthampur_vault|Vanthampur's vault]] to keep him contained. He wants to be freed — and he will say almost anything to manipulate the party into releasing him, including warning them about Zariel.

Freeing Gargauth gives the party a deceptive, dangerous advisor. He provides accurate (but always self-serving) information about Avernus and the Nine Hells.

DM Notes

Gargauth is a great roleplaying opportunity — a devil who tells the truth, but only the truths that serve him. Sylvira at Candlekeep strongly warns against freeing him.
