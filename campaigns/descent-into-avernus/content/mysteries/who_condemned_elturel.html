The Mystery

Who was responsible for Elturel's fall, and how did it happen?

The Truth

[[thavius_kreeg|Thavius Kreeg]], the High Overseer of Elturel, made a secret pact with [[zariel|Zariel]]: in exchange for Zariel driving back a demon horde (which she did, using the Companion), Elturel's entire population would be forfeit to her when the Companion's light went out — 50 years later.

[[duke_thalamra_vanthampur|Duke Thalamra Vanthampur]] knew about the pact and helped ensure it succeeded, hoping to gain power in Baldur's Gate as Elturel's population was consumed.

Evidence Trail

  • [[puzzle_box|Thavius's puzzle box]] contains a copy of the infernal contract
  • [[sylvira_savikas|Sylvira Savikas]] at Candlekeep can help decode it
  • Thavius confesses if cornered before fleeing

DM Notes

The party typically learns this in Ch.1 or early Ch.2.
