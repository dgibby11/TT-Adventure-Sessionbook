What Is the Companion?

The Mystery

For 50 years, a second sun called the Companion hung over Elturel, protecting the city from evil. What is it really?

The Truth

The Companion is an infernal machine — an orb of hellish energy disguised as a holy relic. It was created by [[zariel|Zariel]] as part of her pact with [[thavius_kreeg|Thavius Kreeg]]: drive away the demons threatening Elturel, and in 50 years the Companion's light goes out and drags the entire city to Avernus as payment.

The Companion is the mechanism by which the infernal chains are anchored. Destroying or deactivating it is one possible route to freeing Elturel.

DM Notes

The puzzle box Thavius carried contains details of the contract. Sylvira can decode it.
