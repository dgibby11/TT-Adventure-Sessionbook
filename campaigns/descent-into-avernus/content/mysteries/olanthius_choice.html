Will Olanthius Betray Zariel?

The Mystery

[[olanthius|Olanthius]], a death knight at [[harumanns_hill|Haruman's Hill]], was once a Hellrider who followed [[zariel|Zariel]] into Avernus. He is tormented by guilt. Can he be turned?

The Truth

Olanthius deeply regrets his choice to follow Zariel into hell and is horrified by what he has become. He has found no way to undo the pact that binds him to her. If the party can convince him they intend to redeem Zariel (not just defeat her), he will provide critical intelligence about Avernus, the Bleeding Citadel, and Zariel's weaknesses.

How to Win Him Over

  • Show him [[lulu|Lulu]] (she and Olanthius knew each other)
  • Demonstrate genuine intent to save/redeem Zariel
  • Present evidence of Zariel's former angelic nature

DM Notes

Olanthius won't betray Zariel to have her destroyed. He must believe redemption is possible. He's the adventure's most morally nuanced figure after Zariel herself.
