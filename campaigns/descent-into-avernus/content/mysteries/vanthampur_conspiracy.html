The Vanthampur Conspiracy

The Mystery

Cultists of the Dead Three are murdering people in Baldur's Gate. Who is behind them?

The Truth

[[duke_thalamra_vanthampur|Duke Thalamra Vanthampur]] is funding and directing the Dead Three cultists to destabilize Baldur's Gate. Her goal: seize control of the city in the power vacuum created by Ravengard's disappearance — and ultimately hand Baldur's Gate to [[zariel|Zariel]] as she handed Elturel.

The Vanthampur family home hides a temple to Zariel in its basement and a copy of the infernal contract ([[shield_of_the_hidden_lord|Shield of the Hidden Lord]]) in the vault.
