Main Quest

The Mystery

Can [[zariel|Zariel]] be redeemed — and should she be?

The Truth

Zariel was once a mighty angel of Celestia who volunteered to fight in the Blood War and was eventually overwhelmed and corrupted by Avernus. A fragment of her angelic self remains — locked inside her sword, the [[sword_of_zariel|Sword of Zariel]].

Resolution

If the party retrieves the sword and presents it to Zariel with evidence of her former self (including [[lulu|Lulu]], her old companion), Zariel can be inspired to renounce Asmodeus's pact. This frees Elturel AND transforms Zariel back into an angel — but it requires the party to appeal to her lost conscience rather than defeat her in combat.

DM Notes

Zariel in combat is almost certainly a TPK for levels 11-13. The redemption arc is the intended (but not forced) path. Make the party earn it through roleplay.
