Where Is Duke Ravengard?

The Mystery

Grand Duke Ulder Ravengard of Baldur's Gate was on a diplomatic mission to Elturel when it was dragged into Avernus. Is he alive?

The Answer

[[duke_ulder_ravengard|Ravengard]] is alive — held captive in Elturel by [[zariel|Zariel]]'s forces. Rescuing him is a secondary objective of Chapter 2. If the party saves him, he becomes a powerful ally in Avernus.

He is being held at [[high_hall_elturel|High Hall]] in Elturel, guarded by an amnizu and devil troops.

DM Notes

Ravengard is transformed by his experience in Avernus — once a hard pragmatist, he becomes genuinely humble and grateful. His rescue has meaningful political implications for Baldur's Gate.
