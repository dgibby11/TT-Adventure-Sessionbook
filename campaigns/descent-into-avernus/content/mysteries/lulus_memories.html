What Are Lulu's Lost Memories?

The Mystery

[[lulu|Lulu]] the hollyphant was Zariel's companion before Zariel fell. Her memories were stripped from her. What does she know?

What Lulu Remembers

Lulu's memories unlock gradually as she spends time in Avernus and interacts with key NPCs and locations. She eventually remembers: she witnessed the Battle of Idyllglen (where Zariel first descended into hell), she knows where the [[bleeding_citadel|Bleeding Citadel]] is hidden, and she knows that [[yael|Yael]] is the key to unlocking the Citadel.

How to Trigger Memory Returns

  • Arriving in Avernus (first return)
  • Finding certain Avernus locations (especially [[harumanns_hill|Haruman's Hill]])
  • Meeting [[olanthius|Olanthius]]
  • Reaching [[idyllglen|Idyllglen]] (Ch.4 flashback)

DM Notes

Lulu is the party's compass. Her memory triggers can pace the hexcrawl. Don't rush them.
