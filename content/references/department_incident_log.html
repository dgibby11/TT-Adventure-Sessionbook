Overview

A compiled reference of notable events at each department that were never formally recorded or whose formal records were subsequently altered. Source material for what students half-remember, what faculty will not confirm, and what the administrative record would prefer to not contain. This document is for DM use; it has no in-world correlate as a single source.

Key Incidents by Year

Year 23 — Warlocks Department closure. Official record: "administrative restructuring." Third warlock incident in three cohorts; the third involved a devil's compact that attempted to use Academy grounds as a binding site. The Academy closed the department. Two faculty members who managed the closure did not remain employed afterward. At least one of the bound students is still alive and has visited the campus twice since Year 23, most recently in Year 51, for reasons that are not in any record.

Year 38 — Expedition Club fatality. One student did not return from a field operation in the Nether Mountains. The official report describes weather and terrain as contributing factors. The Expedition Club's internal report, which exists but is not held in administrative files, describes something the team encountered that the weather and terrain notation was added to explain. The [[old_longsword|longsword]] in the Fighters armory is the student's. It should have been in the Nether Mountains.

Year 51 — Sorcerers building incident. An uncontrolled wild surge in the Sorcerers building produced an effect that lasted forty-seven minutes and resulted in a forty-foot section of the second floor being temporarily inaccessible due to spatial distortion. The official record notes "student assessment incident, contained, no injuries." Two faculty were present. One of them now teaches at a different institution. [[prof_ysera|Prof. Ysera]] has a log entry from that day. The entry's anomaly column is longer than any other entry in her decade of documentation.

Pre-Year 23 — Rogues mine previous use. The deep shafts of the Rogues mine were used for something before the department moved in. The records from that period are in the restricted library collection. The use was not academic. It involved regular access, material transport, and at least one incident that is described in a letter from a maintenance worker to the academy administrator in Year 8 as "the thing in the lower level." The thing had apparently been there already when they started using the mine. They apparently continued anyway.

DM Only

Use this log to seed information through NPC conversations, overheard student discussions, and environmental details. None of these incidents need to be revealed at once; they are threads that connect to the main mystery at different distances. The Year 38 incident and the Rogues mine previous use connect most directly to current events. The Year 23 warlock incident and the Year 51 Sorcerers event connect at a higher level — they are evidence that the plane-boundary issue the founders were managing has been producing effects throughout the Academy's history, not just in the present.
