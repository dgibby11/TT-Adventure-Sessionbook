Academy Credits are the official currency of student commerce at FAIL Academy. They are gifted to enrolled students at the start of each academic year and are redeemable exclusively at [[academy_provisions|The Provisions Office]]. They are not gold. The administration is very clear on this point, usually in writing, usually after someone tries to tip a professor.

The Basics

Annual Allotment
100 Academy Credits per enrolled student, issued at the start of each academic year
Value
1 Academy Credit = 1 Gold Piece (purchasing power only — credits cannot be exchanged for gold)
Accepted At
[[academy_provisions|The Provisions Office]] only. No other campus vendor accepts credits.
Expiry
Unused credits expire at end of each academic year. They do not roll over.
Transferability
Credits are officially non-transferable between students. The Academy tracks purchases by student ID.

Healing Potion Allotment

Due to recurring incidents of students stockpiling healing potions and selling them at a markup — and the Academy's stated commitment to "cultivating resilience through managed scarcity" — the purchase of Potions of Healing from The Provisions Office is subject to an annual hard cap.

Store Price
50 Academy Credits each
Annual Cap
2 Potions of Healing per student per academic year
Tracking
[[silas_morne|Silas Morne]] maintains a physical ledger. He does not forget.

Students wishing to purchase additional healing potions beyond the cap must do so through unofficial channels — see below.

Spending Your Credits Wisely

100 credits per year sounds generous until you price out a full adventure kit. Students who spend both their potion allotment (100 credits) have nothing left for arrows, rope, or rations. Most experienced students prioritise supplies over potions and make their own arrangements for healing.

A rough breakdown of common spending patterns:

  • The Cautious Approach: 2 potions (100 AC) — zero supplies, full healing reserve
  • The Practical Kit: 1 potion (50 AC) + arrows + healer's kit + rope + rations (≈50 AC)
  • The Unprepared Student: Spend 80 credits on a spellbook and silk rope, panic in the field

DM Only — The Black Market

Additional healing potions are available through an unofficial student black market. The going rate is 100 gp each — double the store price, gold only, no credits.

Contact: Students in the know mention asking around after hours at [[food_court|the Food Court]], specifically near the serving hatch on the east wall. The broker changes weekly. Current broker is a third-year Bard named Oryn Saltwick who operates under the protection of [[the_upperclassmen|The Upper-Classmen]] — they take a cut and ensure the supply chain stays quiet.

The Academy knows the black market exists. They have chosen not to shut it down. No official record of why.

DM Only — Credit System Hooks

  • Credits are "non-transferable" but [[silas_morne|Silas]] has quietly allowed a handful of exceptions over the years. He will not discuss them.
  • The [[thornwick_consortium|Thornwick Consortium]] is believed to have access to anonymised credit spending records — what they do with this information is unknown but likely involves identifying students under financial pressure as potential recruits or marks.
  • A student can theoretically exhaust their credits in the first week of term. Some do. It is a rite of passage of a sort.
  • Credits were introduced 18 years ago under [[lenny_goldvein|Lenny Goldvein's]] financial restructuring. Before that, the store ran on gold and operated at a significant loss. The restructuring was publicly framed as a student benefit.
