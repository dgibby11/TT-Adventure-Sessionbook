Overview

The official campus map used by the administration for maintenance scheduling, security routing, and facility management. It is accurate for the main campus buildings and central facilities. It is not accurate for everything else.

Known Omissions

The map does not show: the Rogues mine entrance (listed as "eastern ridge, undeveloped"); the north wall sealed gate (the north wall is shown as solid); the East Annex sub-basement (the East Annex is shown with ground floor only); the full extent of the [[underground_storage|underground storage]] system (shown terminating two sections before the actual limit).

Two of these omissions — the mine entrance and the underground storage extent — appear to be the result of the map never being updated. The other two omissions are structural: the north wall and the East Annex sub-basement appear in an earlier map, held in the [[restricted_floor|restricted library collection]], and were removed from this version in Year 47. The removal was made by hand on the original document. The person who made the removal wrote their initials in the margin, then scratched them out.

The Initialed Scratching

The initials are partially legible. The first letter is C or G. The second is unclear. The action of scratching out an initial on an administrative document is not something people do carelessly — it suggests the person changed their mind about leaving the record after they'd already started to.

DM Only

The initials belong to an administrator from Year 47 who was, at that point, cooperating with the [[secret_society|Secret Society]] and who had second thoughts mid-action. The administrator left the Academy in Year 49 "for personal reasons" and has not been in contact since. The map with the scratched initials is in the main administrative files. Players who access these files and examine the map closely — Investigation DC 13 — can identify the partial initials and recognize that the removal was deliberate rather than a drafting omission.
