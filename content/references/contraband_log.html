Overview

The Campus Security log of confiscated items, maintained personally by [[captain_hadra|Captain Hadra]] since Year 45. Comprehensive and accurately dated. Covers contraband recovered from student possession, items confiscated at perimeter, and materials surrendered by students in the context of security interviews. The log is the most accurate record of what has been physically moving around the campus outside official channels.

The Redacted Entries

Three entries in the last two years have been redacted at a level above Hadra's authority. Not removed from the log — the entry dates are present, the officer-on-duty notation is present, the format is otherwise standard. The item descriptions have been replaced with: "Removed per administrative directive." Hadra has no record of which administrative authority issued the directive. The directives did not come through her office. She noticed this on the day the redactions appeared in the log she keeps, which means someone with access to the log made the changes between her last review and the following morning.

Hadra's Response

She documented the redactions in a separate file, which she does not keep in her office. She has not filed a formal complaint because the only thing a formal complaint would produce is a formal response, which she expects would be less informative than what she already knows. She has not closed the matter. She has set it aside until she has more to work with.

DM Only

The three redacted items were: the [[founders_compass|nine-pointed compass]], recovered from a student in Year 54; a sealed document that matched the description of a Founding-era authorization credential; and a small locked box that Hadra's officer noted as "weight inconsistent with size, possible magical contents." The compass was returned to the [[secret_society|Secret Society]] through the administrative directive. The document and the box have not been located. Whoever issued the directive knew what the items were and specifically wanted the compass back — the other two items were collateral management.

Hadra's separate file contains her notes from the officer who logged the original entries. That officer is no longer employed at the Academy — not fired, transferred to an external posting. The transfer was arranged quickly. Hadra has noted the timing. She has not contacted the transferred officer yet. She's waiting to see if the players give her a reason to.
