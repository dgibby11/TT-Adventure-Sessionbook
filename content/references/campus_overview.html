FAIL Academy is a magical combat academy designed to train students in various forms of combat, magic, and specialized disciplines. The academy is organized into distinct departments, each housing faculty and students dedicated to specific schools of magic and combat styles. It features both above-ground structures and extensive underground infrastructure, though much of what lies beneath the surface remains unknown to the general student body.

The physical campus is laid out as a sprawling complex surrounded by wilderness, with multiple interconnected buildings arranged around a central courtyard. A river runs along the western edge of the property. The campus includes dedicated facilities for each of the twelve major training disciplines, support structures (such as a food court and administrative buildings), and hidden systems of underground chambers and storage areas.

Geographic Context

Position
South of the River Rauvin
Northern boundary
Nether Mountains visible on the horizon; ~12 miles to the foothills
River crossing
The River Rauvin lies between campus and the mountains; crossed by ferry or ford depending on season
Surrounding terrain
Wilderness on most sides; forest adjacent to the southwest (near Rangers)

The campus was deliberately situated with the river as a natural northern boundary and the mountains as a visual backdrop.

DM Only

Founding records suggest this positioning was chosen by Aldric Voss for reasons beyond aesthetics — the elevated terrain to the north, and the river as a ward boundary, appear in early architectural diagrams annotated with containment notation.
