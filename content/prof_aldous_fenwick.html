Appearance

Professor Aldous Fenwick is a very small gnome with wispy white hair that he brushes in three different directions with uncertain result. He wears the same green-grey academic robes he has worn since approximately the Academy's founding, and they smell of chalk dust, old paper, and something faintly chemical that no one has been able to identify. He speaks at a consistent, unhurried speed regardless of whether anyone is listening.

Teaching Style

Fenwick's lecture notes were last updated thirty-one years ago. Some of the spellcasting theory he teaches was deprecated twenty years ago, but he teaches it with complete confidence. His exam questions reference magical phenomena that have since been reclassified, renamed, or in two cases proven never to have existed. Students who answer his questions correctly based on current scholarship receive partial credit. Students who answer his questions using his own outdated framework receive full marks.

He gives no partial credit for effort, attendance, or obvious distress. He does give extra credit for citing his own research papers, of which there are forty-three, all written before most current students' parents were born.

The Paradox

Students are consistently baffled to discover, usually in their second year, that Fenwick's foundational theory — despite being out of date — is occasionally the only thing standing between them and a catastrophic misapplication of practical magic. Something about his antiquated framework produces graduates who make fewer fatal errors. No one has a satisfying explanation for this.

DM Only

Fenwick is not stupid. He knows his curriculum is out of date. He has reasons for teaching it this way that he has never explained to anyone, including [[headmistress_dowe|Headmistress Dowe]], who has asked three times. His reasons relate to something he discovered in Year 34 — the [[academy_legends|Lost Year]] — and which he believes makes a certain kind of theoretical sloppiness extremely dangerous.

He has not said what he discovered. He is a gnome. He has time.

Hook: Fenwick can, if pressed with the right question, direct PCs to resources in the [[old_library|Old Library]] that have been misfiled — not accidentally. He doesn't help willingly. He helps when he decides someone has asked the right question, and he will not explain what made it right.
