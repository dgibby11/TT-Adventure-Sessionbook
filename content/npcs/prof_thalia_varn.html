Appearance

Professor Thalia Varn is a dwarf woman with close-cropped silver hair and a scar that runs from her chin to her left ear, origin never confirmed. She is compact and solid and gives the impression that she has been in enough fights to stop being impressed by most things. She wears leather training armor to every class, including administrative meetings. The Academic Council has mentioned the dress code. She has attended five such meetings in armor. She will attend all future meetings in armor.

Teaching Style

Varn teaches by showing and by pushing. Her practical sessions are physically demanding, tactically rigorous, and she has an uncanny ability to find exactly the one thing a student is avoiding and make them practice it. Her rule is simple: show up, try hard, and she will go to war for you. Fail to show up or give up mid-session, and she will not say a word — but she will not have forgotten.

She gives partial credit for creative failure. She gives full credit for creative failure that nearly worked. She has passed students who lost every practical exam but showed up to every session.

She runs the [[dueling_society|Arcane Dueling Society]] as faculty advisor, mostly by showing up once a month, watching quietly, and saying "again" after each bout.

DM Only

Varn came to the Academy after fifteen years of mercenary work in the Western Heartlands. She knows more about what field combat actually looks like than anyone else on the faculty. She also knows that the Academy's "field exercises" have been getting more dangerous and less supervised over the past six years, and she has raised this with [[headmistress_dowe|Headmistress Dowe]] three times. Each time, she has been told it is under review.

She is not part of any conspiracy. She suspects there is one. She is keeping her head down while quietly making sure her students can handle whatever they run into. If the PCs earn her trust, she's a useful source of tactical advice and one of the few faculty who will tell them the truth about what they're walking into.

Hook: She has a detailed list of every student injury and death in field exercises for the past twelve years. She keeps it because no one official is keeping it. She will share it if she trusts the recipient.
