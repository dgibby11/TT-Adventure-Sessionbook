A diminutive goblin with ink-stained fingers, perpetually disheveled robes, and wire-rimmed spectacles. Speaks with pedantic precision and corrects grammar constantly — even mid-crisis. Obsessed with proper cataloging and documentation. Has led at least one student expedition to the [[nether_mountains|Nether Mountains]] under the guise of academic component gathering.

Access to his tower is controlled — a letter of introduction from a senior faculty member is required for non-students to enter. Catchphrases: "Precisely!" and "Now, where was I?"

DM Only

The true purpose of the Nether Mountains expedition was a supply run for the [[thornwick_consortium|Thornwick Arcane Consortium]], a prestigious arcane university three weeks' travel east. Crumb is a point of contact between the Academy and outside arcane powers. He views hired party members as "bodyguards," not colleagues — not malicious, just genuinely oblivious to the stakes he imposes on others.

Despite his shabby appearance, Crumb is very wealthy — gold concealed in various pouches and a lockbox he never lets leave his sight. He is a terrible liar and will eventually admit everything if pressed. His scattered demeanor masks a shrewd businessman who knows exactly what his components are worth.

The Consortium's Nether Mountains research site has been attacked twice in the past six months by orc warbands; Crumb reported both incidents to the Consortium, not to Academy administration.
