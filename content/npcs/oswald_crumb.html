Professor of Arcane Studies
Associated with
Crumb's tower, near the [[wizards|Wizards Department]]

A senior arcane faculty member. Access to his tower is controlled — a letter of introduction from a senior faculty member (e.g., Dr. Voss) is required for non-students to enter. He has led at least one student expedition to the [[nether_mountains|Nether Mountains]] under the guise of academic component gathering.

DM Only

The true purpose of the Nether Mountains expedition was a supply run for the [[thornwick_consortium|Thornwick Arcane Consortium]]. Crumb is a point of contact between the academy and outside arcane powers.
