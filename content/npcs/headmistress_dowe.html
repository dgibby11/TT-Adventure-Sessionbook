Appearance

Headmistress Seranthia Dowe is a tiefling of moderate height with deep burgundy skin, small swept-back horns, and amber eyes that seem to read whatever room she walks into before she finishes walking into it. She dresses in black with a deep red academic sash bearing the Academy crest. She moves quietly. Students often notice she has arrived before they saw her enter.

Public Role

Dowe is the public face and administrative authority of FAIL Academy. She chairs the Academic Council, presides over [[academy_traditions|all major ceremonies]], signs every expulsion, and personally reads every disciplinary report. She gives a speech at the start of each academic year that is exactly seventeen minutes long. It has never been the same speech twice, and students who pay close attention notice it gets a little darker each year.

She is scrupulously fair. Students who go to her with genuine grievances find she listens carefully and acts. Students who try to manipulate her find the conversation ends much faster than they expected.

Personality

Dowe is formal but not cold. She is precise but not unkind. She finds humor in unexpected places and does not broadcast it. She has an unsettling habit of answering questions that weren't quite asked.

She knows [[lenny_goldvein|Lenny Goldvein]] is cutting corners somewhere and has not yet decided what to do about it. She has a respectful but wary relationship with [[ellery_voss|Dr. Ellery Voss]] that she describes as "collegial."

DM Only

Dowe knows the [[secret_society|Secret Society]] exists. She has known since her second year in post. She has not acted to dismantle it because she has spent twenty years carefully managing what she believes is a contained situation — and because two previous administrators who tried to dismantle it directly are no longer in any position to say whether that was a wise approach.

She is not complicit in the Society's worst activities. She is complicit in her own silence, which she justifies as harm-reduction. This distinction matters to her enormously. Whether it holds up is a question for later in the campaign.

Using Dowe: She can be an ally, an antagonist, or (ideally) both at different moments. If PCs bring her credible evidence of what's happening below the school, she will act — but on her own terms and timeline, not theirs. She is the most powerful individual in the building. She is also the most isolated.

Secret: She has a complete record of the "Lost Year" (Year 34 — see [[academy_legends]]). She keeps it in a locked box in her private quarters, not her office. She has read it twice and doesn't intend to read it again.
