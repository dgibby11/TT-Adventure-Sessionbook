Role
Second of the three original founders — author of the charter
Status
Disappeared

Orath authored the founding [[orath_charter|charter]], which specified non-discriminatory admission, perpetual scholarship funding, and a prohibition on requiring students to demonstrate abilities in circumstances designed to harm them. That prohibition now exists in policy in language so qualified as to be nearly unrecognizable from its original form.

Three letters from Orath to [[brennan_ashcroft]], written in the months before her disappearance, are held under administrative seal in the [[old_library|restricted archive]]. They have not been made available for research. Their contents are unknown.
