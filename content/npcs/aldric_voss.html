Role
One of the three original founders — architect of the campus
Descendant
[[ellery_voss|Dr. Ellery Voss]]

Voss designed the wards, containment systems, underground vaults, and examination chambers. His [[voss_imprint|architectural imprint]] is everywhere on campus — layered sealing glyphs, tiered containment, the lower research wings.

Founding records note that Voss built rooms before uses were assigned to them. Some were sealed before the academy's first students arrived and remain sealed. Whatever he was building for, he was building for more than a school.

His tomb lies in the [[memorial_garden|Founder's Memorial Garden]] — a modest marker, name and dates only. The tomb is empty. This is not publicly known.

RESTRICTED: The empty tomb is a discovery point. Do not surface it until players actively investigate the garden.
