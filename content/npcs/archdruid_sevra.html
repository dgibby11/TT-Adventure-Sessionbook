Overview

Archdruid Sevra has been at the Academy since Year 12, which makes her the second-longest-serving faculty member after records that may be incomplete. She arrived as an instructor and has not had a different title since, which she appears to consider adequate.

Plants move toward her when she enters a room. She does not appear to notice. Students have pointed it out. She nods in the way of someone being told something they already knew and did not consider interesting.

Teaching

She teaches through immersion. Students spend more time in the [[druids_grove|inner grove]] than in any classroom, and the distinction between the two is, she maintains, artificial. Curriculum in the Druids Department does not distinguish between "field work" and "study" because the field is where study happens. Students who arrive expecting lectures leave after the first month. Students who stay are not students who came expecting lectures.

Campus Relationships

She coordinates with the [[rangers|Rangers Department]] on the seasonal behaviors of campus wildlife, particularly the [[high_forest_elk|elk herd]] that crosses the grounds each autumn. She has a working relationship with [[torvald_thatch|Torvald Thatch]] that predates most current faculty appointments. Neither of them explains what they talk about when they're seen together at the edge of the grove.

She knows the [[campus_ravens|campus ravens]] individually. This is not something she has explained.

DM Only

Sevra knows which of the campus cats are familiars and whose. She has not told anyone. The list includes one familiar belonging to a student who graduated in Year 48 and has not been back since — the familiar is still here, still present, which she takes as information about its bonded person. Whether that information is good or bad she hasn't determined.

The [[grove_spirit|grove spirit]] in the inner courtyard predates the Academy. It was here when the Druids Department chose this location, and it permitted the choice. Sevra has known this since approximately Year 15. The spirit communicates through the behavior of plants and, occasionally, through the orientation of things that aren't plants. She has been in dialogue with it for several decades. She considers this a professional relationship, not a mystical one.
