Appearance

Torvald Thatch is a broad half-orc in his late sixties who has the unhurried movement of someone who has walked the same grounds so many times he has stopped thinking about walking them. He carries a lantern after dark regardless of weather, wears a canvas coat in every season, and knows the name of every student who's been here more than two weeks. He doesn't use names to be friendly — he uses them to let you know you've been noticed.

Role

His official responsibilities include grounds maintenance, minor structural repairs, gate management after hours, and reporting anything unusual to the Administrative Wing. His actual responsibilities, accumulated over four decades, include a great deal more. He is the person who knows where the old pipes run, which doors can be opened from the outside even when locked, which areas of campus maintenance has officially abandoned and why, and what the grounds looked like before the administration decided certain things weren't worth noting on maps.

Torvald is not on the Academic Council. He is not in the faculty directory. He is not in any formal chain of command that a student or outside observer would find. He has keys to every door on campus, including several that are not on the official key registry.

Personality

Direct. Economical. He does not explain himself to people who haven't earned an explanation, and he determines who has earned one by criteria he keeps to himself. Students who ask him genuine questions tend to get genuine answers — shorter than they hoped, more useful than they expected. Students who ask him leading questions find the conversation over before they finish the setup.

He trusts the [[campus_ravens|ravens]]. He has opinions about the [[memorial_garden|garden]] that he does not share. He knows [[headmistress_dowe|Headmistress Dowe]] by first name and calls her Seranthia when they are not in front of students, which has happened exactly once within student earshot and was never discussed.

What He Knows

Forty-one years of walking the same campus at night produces a particular kind of knowledge. Torvald has seen things that aren't in the academic record, and he is the kind of person who noticed. Whether he'll share what he noticed depends on who's asking and why, and he is difficult to lie to about the why.

DM Only

Torvald has seen someone enter the [[eastern_wing|Eastern Wing]] through the sealed corridor. Multiple times, over multiple decades, multiple different someones. He does not know who they are. He has never attempted to stop them. He has never reported it. He filed it alongside a number of other things he has observed over forty-one years under the mental heading of "none of my business to interfere with, but mine to know."

He has a journal. It is not in the groundskeeper's shed. He knows the [[underground_storage|basement system]] better than anyone currently employed by the Academy, having been the person who reported the original access issues to the administration in Year 3 of his tenure and been told, very clearly, to stop checking. He stopped checking. He did not stop noticing.

As an ally: Torvald will not volunteer information. He will confirm things players already know if they ask correctly. He will not lie to them. He will tell them when a question isn't his to answer, which is different from refusing — and players who are paying attention will notice the difference.
