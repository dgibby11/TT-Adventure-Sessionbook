Founded
The Alumni Society — now the [[secret_society|Secret Society]]

Ashcroft founded what was originally presented as an alumni networking and institutional-support body. Its student-facing identity today is the [[secret_society|Secret Society]].

The original founding documents of the Alumni Society were removed from the [[old_library|restricted archive]] at some point during the academy's second century — after the founding generation was dead. What they contained is not known. What replaced them is not documented.

Ashcroft's descendants continue to hold seats on the academy's board of governors, without interruption, across the full institutional history.
