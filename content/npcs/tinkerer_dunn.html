Overview

Tinkerer Dunn arrived at the Academy in Year 44 as a guest lecturer and has not left. At some point in Year 45 he was given the department head title, which he took as permission to order more materials. He has six active projects at all times — the exact projects rotate, but the number is consistent in a way that appears deliberate. He has never explained why six.

Teaching

He teaches by involving students in his current projects, which means students learn fabrication, magical integration, and failure analysis simultaneously and somewhat involuntarily. The [[artificers_forge|forge]] is always running. The schedule is one that only senior students understand and that Dunn himself describes as "responsive." Three different things have caught fire this semester. Two of them were supposed to.

Knowledge Gaps

He sources materials through [[academy_provisions|Provisions]] and through personal supplier networks he has maintained since before the Academy. He does not know that three of his former students are currently employed by the [[thornwick_consortium|Thornwick Consortium]]. He would have opinions about this if he found out — he has opinions about Thornwick's approach to arcane fabrication that he has expressed at length to anyone who will stand still — but he does not know.

DM Only

The [[alchemy_notebook|notebook]] found in the forge is not Dunn's. He is aware it exists and does not know who left it. He has looked at it twice and each time concluded he couldn't determine what it was calibrating toward. This is one of three things on campus he cannot figure out, and it bothers him in proportion to how unusual that is for him. He has not reported it because he is not certain it is a problem and is reluctant to flag something to administration before he understands it. This has been going on for eight months.
