Presented to students as a magical examination chamber used for practical testing.

DM Only

Actual function: a controlled teleportation gateway to remote field sites.

Students participated in a practical FAIL midterm presented as a jungle retrieval exercise. The chamber actually teleported them to a real [[jungle_site|remote field site]] hundreds of miles away. During extraction, a [[dinosaurs|Triceratops]] breached the campus portal, causing significant facility damage before being killed by faculty.

Dr. Voss is a descendant of founder [[aldric_voss]]. She demonstrates an unusual degree of independence in her professional judgment relative to other senior faculty. The reason for this is not currently understood.
