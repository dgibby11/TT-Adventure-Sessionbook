Overview

The central administrative tower flanked by two academic wings and a formal courtyard. The Gothic stonework of the original sections sits alongside newer administrative additions that make no architectural effort to match their surroundings. Students who study architecture or simply pay attention can date the expansion phases of the campus's history from the outside of this building alone.

The [[academy_provisions|Provisions Office]] occupies a ground-floor shopfront in the south wing. The [[old_library|Old Library]] is accessible through the eastern courtyard.

Access and Bureaucracy

The Academic Council chamber is where all formal decisions above department level are made — curriculum changes, expulsions, budget allocations, and the periodic review of which parts of campus are officially "accessible" to students and which are not. Minutes from council meetings are maintained in Records. Minutes from the last three decades are complete. Minutes before that have significant gaps in Year 47 and nothing after Year 89.

Students who need to interact with the administrative wing quickly learn that the most efficient path to any outcome involves correctly identifying which office is actually responsible for the decision they need, which is rarely the obvious one.

The Headmistress's Study

[[headmistress_dowe|Dowe's]] office is on the top floor. The view from the window covers the entire main courtyard, the Founder's Statue, and three department rooftops. Students who have been there report that she always seems to have known they were coming before they knocked.

DM Only

The second-floor administrative wing contains a door with no nameplate, no outside handle, and a mail slot that appears and disappears. This is the Academic Council's secure communications room, used for correspondence with external parties who are not in the campus directory. Who those parties are is not in any file accessible without Dowe's authorization.

The eastern annex — the oldest-looking part of the building — predates the main tower and appears on no founding blueprint. It is connected to the [[eastern_wing|Eastern Wing]] by a corridor that has been painted over and is not visible without removing the plaster.
