Overview

The Rogues mine has charted sections and uncharted sections. The charted sections appear in the department's internal navigation guides, used by students during their first two years to learn the layout. Beyond the charted sections, the notation in [[tavin_crestfall|Crestfall's]] field journal — the most comprehensive current documentation of the deep shafts — shifts. The marking conventions become inconsistent, then unfamiliar, then absent. The navigation at depth is not recorded in any system that students recognize.

The Shafts

The deep shafts are original mine workings — the miners who abandoned this site went further down than the sections the department uses, and further down than anyone currently enrolled has been. The passages narrow and widen unpredictably. The air is cooler and carries a faint mineral smell that changes at certain junctions. Things move in the dark that are not students. They have not been aggressive. They have not been identified.

The Permanent Tenant

[[tavin_crestfall|Crestfall's]] journal refers to something in the deep shafts as "the permanent tenant." The reference appears three times. No description is given in any of the three instances. The size is unknown. The journal is currently down here, in the section where it went missing. Its last entry is mid-sentence.

DM Only

The permanent tenant is a creature that has been in the mine for longer than the Academy. It predates the miners. It was here when the miners arrived, and they left not because the ore ran out but because they encountered something in the lower levels that persuaded them the ore was not worth the problem. It has not harmed students because it does not consider students significant enough to address. Crestfall mapped around it because he is intelligent enough to recognize that "the permanent tenant" is an entity that has made a decision to coexist and that this arrangement is preferable to the alternative.

The [[mine_crawlers|mine crawlers]] are something related to this creature — either its offspring, its companions, or something else that shares its space by the same tolerance that the creature extends to students. The creature is aware of the [[ritual_floor|ritual floor]] below the main campus and has opinions about what is happening there that it has not been asked and cannot be asked without a specific kind of approach that no current student or faculty member knows.
