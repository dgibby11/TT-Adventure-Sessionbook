Overview

The headmistress's working office is on the second floor of the Main Complex, reached through an outer reception area managed by [[ambrose_quill|Ambrose Quill]]. Students and faculty with appointments enter the outer office and wait. The inner office — where Dowe's actual desk, files, and working materials are — requires being invited through a second door.

The inner office has large windows facing the main courtyard. The desk is clear except for the current working file. The bookshelves are organized and accessible. A locked drawer in the lower right is the only visibly secured object in the room. The room is tidier than a room in active use usually is, which several students have noted as slightly unsettling without being able to explain why.

Function

Meetings with Dowe happen here — academic councils, faculty disputes, student discipline hearings, and occasionally something that is described on Quill's schedule as "administrative review" and does not have a student or faculty name attached. The outer office records all appointments. The inner office records are Dowe's.

DM Only

The locked drawer contains Dowe's personal correspondence with her predecessor and the complete record of the Lost Year (Year 34). She has read it twice and does not intend to read it again. The drawer itself is not magically secured — just a standard lock with a key she keeps on her person. Breaking into it is physically feasible. What's inside is the kind of information that changes how several other people on campus need to be approached.

The "administrative review" appointments are meetings with individuals who have information she needs to manage. Three of the five people who have had such appointments in the last year are people the players may encounter. None of them have voluntarily described what the meeting was about. One of them looked notably less comfortable after it than before.
