[[aldric_voss|Aldric Voss]] designed the wards, containment systems, underground vaults, and examination chambers. His work appears in every corner of the physical campus — the underground storage vaults, the tiered containment systems in the lower research wings, and the examination chambers with their layered sealing glyphs all bear his design.

Founding records note that Voss built rooms before uses were assigned to them. Some were sealed before the academy's first students arrived and remain sealed. Whatever Voss was building for, he was building for more than a school.

Voss's architectural diagrams show underground chambers and vaults that do not appear in current facility documentation. These may still exist beneath the campus. Their contents are unknown.
