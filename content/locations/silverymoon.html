Overview

Silverymoon is the nearest major city to FAIL Academy and the primary destination for students seeking goods, services, or civilisation unavailable on campus. The city has a well-earned reputation as the most cosmopolitan settlement in the North — a hub of arcane scholarship, artistic patronage, and cautious political alliance.

The [[river_rauvin|River Rauvin]] flows from the Nether Mountains past the Academy campus and continues southwest to Silverymoon. Students with access to a boat can reach the city in two days downriver. The overland route takes three to four days depending on conditions and encounters near the [[high_forest|High Forest]] edge.

What Students Go There For

  • The Market District: Goods unavailable at [[academy_provisions|The Provisions Office]] or in quantities the Academy won't supply. Anything from additional spell components to non-standard weapons to books the Academy library doesn't stock.
  • The Vault of Stars: Silverymoon's primary arcane library and research archive. Open to accredited scholars — FAIL Academy credentials are accepted, though some restricted sections require additional clearance from city authorities.
  • Healing temples: The Temple of Mielikki and the Shrine of Selûne both offer healing services. Useful for injuries the Academy's infirmary has been slow to acknowledge.
  • The Moonbridge taverns: Three taverns within sight of the Moonbridge have become informal FAIL Academy alumni hangouts. The oldest graduates, the ones who come back, come here.

Relevant Factions in Silverymoon

  • The Spellguard — Silverymoon's elite magical military force. Maintain order, regulate dangerous magic use, and are politely but firmly interested in anything involving entities or artifacts from the surrounding wilderness.
  • The Harpers — Active in Silverymoon. Students who demonstrate competence and the right ethical alignment occasionally attract attention. Not always welcome attention.

DM Only

The [[thornwick_consortium|Thornwick Consortium]] has a trading office in Silverymoon's Market District — "Gravelstone & Associates, Arcane Procurement" — which serves as their above-ground front operation. It is how they move items like the ones currently in [[academy_provisions|The Prov's]] Special Acquisitions cabinet. PCs who follow the paper trail from the store could eventually reach this office.

Two FAIL Academy graduates currently serve in the Spellguard. One of them knows something about Year 34. She has not told her superiors. She is waiting to see if anyone asks the right question.
