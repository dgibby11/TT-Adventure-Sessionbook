Overview

The armory is behind the training yard, through a reinforced door that has been locked with the same key for at least fifteen years. It holds the department's real equipment — not the training weapons, which are racked in the practice yard corridor, but functional weapons, protective gear, and materials used for advanced assessments and field work. The inventory is conducted annually. It has been accurate to within one item for the past six years.

The Inventory Anomaly

Several items in the armory predate [[prof_thalia_varn|Prof. Varn's]] tenure by several decades. There is no record of how they entered inventory. They appear in the annual count with no acquisition date in the ledger. They are labeled with codes that the current cataloguing system does not use and that Varn cannot match to any system the Academy has formally employed. She has noted this in the inventory records each year. She has not received a response to those notes.

DM Only

The [[old_longsword|unregistered longsword]] on the far rack is one of these items, though it arrived more recently — it appeared in the armory in Year 38, which is the same year as the Expedition Club fatality. Varn knows it's there. She has not moved it, registered it, or reported it, because she concluded approximately six years ago that reporting unexplained weapons in the armory to the administration produces no response and may produce the wrong kind of attention. She keeps the key and waits for someone to claim it or explain it. No one has.

The [[drill_notes|drill notes]] are also here — folded inside a leather document case on the top shelf, behind the ceremonial gear. Varn has read them once. She does not use the exercises described. She hasn't thrown them out.
