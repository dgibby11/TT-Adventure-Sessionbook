Location
Central campus — adjacent to the Founder's Memorial Garden

The old library is the original academic archive of the institution, predating the current administrative complex. It contains historical records, academic texts, and restricted materials. Most students use newer facilities and pass through this area without attention.

DM Only

The library's restricted archive holds sealed documents from the founding period, including correspondence between the three founders. Several have been placed under administrative seal and are not available for general research. Requests for access are not approved without dean-level authorization.

One surviving institutional history notes that the restricted archive contained materials that were "administratively withdrawn" at some point during the academy's second century of operation — after the founding generation was long dead.
