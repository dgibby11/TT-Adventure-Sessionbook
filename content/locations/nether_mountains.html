Location
Nether Mountains — ~12 miles north of campus, across the River Rauvin
Access
Overland — 12-mile journey, crossing the River Rauvin by ferry or ford
Terrain
Rough ascending terrain through mountain foothills; temperature drops sharply at night
Known resources
Crystallized bat guano (shallow caves), petrified fungal spores (sedimentary rock), powdered elemental granite (high altitude), minor components throughout

[[oswald_crumb|Professor Oswald Crumb]] (Arcane Studies) has led at least one student expedition to this site under the guise of academic component gathering.

DM Only

The true purpose of Crumb's expedition was a supply run for the [[thornwick_consortium|Thornwick Arcane Consortium]].

A blue dragon has been reported in proximity to the cave systems in this area. This has not been officially investigated.

The mountains contain cave systems of uncertain depth. An Earth Elemental presence has been documented — triggered by excavation activity, particularly in the granite-rich zones, and associated with falling-rock environmental hazards.
