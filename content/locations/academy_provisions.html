The Provisions Office occupies the ground-floor east wing of the Main Academy Complex, identifiable by the hand-lettered sign above the door that reads "Office of Academic Supply & Provisions — Authorised Students Only" and the smaller, unofficial sign below it that reads "Yes, we know you want more potions. The answer is still no." That second sign has been there for eleven years. No one has claimed responsibility for it. [[silas_morne|Silas Morne]] has not removed it.

Students call it The Prov. Most faculty pretend not to know this nickname, except [[lenny_goldvein|Lenny Goldvein]], who uses it himself and seems genuinely pleased that it caught on.

Layout

  • Back Room (staff only) — stockroom and Silas's desk; students are never invited in

The Bulletin Board

A cork board beside the main entrance holds a rotating collection of notices:

  • "LOST: One (1) standard-issue quiver, initials R.T. carved in the leather. Reward: 5 AC. Ask at Room 14-B."
  • "STUDY GROUP forming for Advanced Conjuration practical. All years welcome. Serious applicants only — we lost two people last term."
  • "The Restricted Section of the [[old_library|Old Library]] is CLOSED until further notice by order of the Academic Council. This is not a drill. Do not attempt to enter."
  • "REMINDER: The East Campus exit after 10pm requires a faculty escort or a FAIL Expedition Clearance Badge. Students found in the perimeter without authorisation will be reported."

DM Only

The back room: Silas's desk contains his official ledger, three years of supply requisition forms, and — inside a false-bottomed drawer — his private record of purchase anomalies he cannot explain. See [[silas_morne|Silas's entry]] for full detail. The back room door is locked (DC 14 Thieves' Tools or Silas's key).

After-hours access: The Prov has a standard lock (DC 12). The supply racks are unguarded. The Special Acquisitions cabinet is a quality lock (DC 17) with an alarm glyph (Perception DC 16 to spot; triggers a silent alert to Silas's quarters, not the faculty watch). Breaking in is possible but Silas will notice inventory discrepancies within one day.

The Hat of Disguise: It's not on back-order. Silas sold it three weeks ago off the books to a faculty member who paid cash and asked him not to log it. He will not volunteer this information but will not lie if directly asked.

[[thornwick_consortium|Thornwick Consortium]] connection: The "Special Acquisitions" supplier is a Thornwick shell operation called Gravelstone Recovery. Silas does not know this. The provenance documentation on all three current items is real but incomplete.
