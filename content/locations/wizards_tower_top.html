Overview

The seventh floor of the Wizards Tower is not listed in the campus directory. The building's directory, posted inside the main entrance, numbers floors one through six. The external architecture is consistent with seven floors. Students who have counted the windows have noted the discrepancy. Faculty who have been asked about it describe the seventh floor as "storage." There is no storage record for it.

Access

The door to the seventh floor stair landing is sealed with a ward that does not appear in the campus maintenance register — which tracks all active magical fixtures, theoretically. The ward is functional. It has been checked by students who know enough to identify an active ward without triggering it. The smell of burning that runs through the Wizards Tower generally is strongest on this floor, detectable through the door.

Known Traffic

Three senior faculty members have been observed entering the stair toward the seventh floor in the last year. None of them used the standard campus key system; all three used credentials that were not visible in any conventional sense. [[ellery_voss|Ellery Voss]] had access. Whether she still does is unconfirmed.

The seventh floor contains the primary observation node for the campus's underlying enchantment system — the network of resonance points, monitoring wards, and legacy infrastructure that the founders installed and that no one currently employed fully understands. The burning smell is residual from a Year 51 incident in which someone attempted to access the observation node without proper credentials and the ward responded. The attempt failed. The burn marks on the floor and one wall are still there. The person who made the attempt has not been identified from the outside.

Voss had access because her great-great-grandfather [[aldric_voss|Aldric Voss]] built the access credential into the original ward. The [[voss_keycard|keycard]] with her initials opens the stair ward — not a door, but the ward itself, which accepts the card as a secondary credential below the primary magical authentication. She has not been back since Year 52 and her keycard is currently unaccounted for.
