Overview

Everlund is the less glamorous, considerably more commercial sibling of [[silverymoon|Silverymoon]]. Where Silverymoon is a city of scholarship and culture, Everlund is a city of trade, caravans, and pragmatic decision-making about who you let through your gate. It sits at a key crossing point on the River Rauvin and serves as the primary gateway between the Silver Marches and the trade routes east.

FAIL Academy students are less likely to visit Everlund than Silverymoon — it's farther and has fewer arcane resources — but it's a vital route for anyone travelling to or from the east, and a useful source of goods that Silverymoon's merchants consider beneath them.

Relevance to the Academy

  • Supply chains: Several of the Academy's bulk supply contracts route through Everlund traders. This includes the food supply contracts. And, on at least one account in [[silas_morne|Silas Morne's]] private ledger, certain "Specimen Containment — Biological" orders.
  • Mercenary recruitment: Everlund has an active mercenary guild, the Swords of Everlund. Students who have completed the Academy curriculum and want paid field work sometimes end up here.
  • Information: The caravan routes through Everlund carry news from the east that reaches Silverymoon only days later. Merchants at the Caravan Quarter often know things that haven't reached official channels.

The Everlund Bridge

The Everlund Bridge is the primary crossing point over the River Rauvin for this region — reinforced stone, guarded, toll-charging, and maintained by the city authority. Control of the bridge is why Everlund exists where it does and why it continues to matter despite having none of Silverymoon's cultural prestige. Several historical conflicts in the Silver Marches have been decided by who controlled the bridge.

DM Only

The "Specimen Containment — Biological" orders that [[silas_morne|Silas]] noted route through a Everlund trader called Harwick Provisions. Harwick Provisions is, like Gravelstone Recovery in Silverymoon, a [[thornwick_consortium|Thornwick Consortium]] front. The biological specimens are not being delivered to any lab the Academy officially recognises. If PCs trace the supply chain, Everlund is step three in the chain (Academy → Silverymoon → Everlund → somewhere east).
