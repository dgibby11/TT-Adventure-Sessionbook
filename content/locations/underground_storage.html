The Bodak

A Medium undead creature with devastating abilities. Its Death Gaze can instantly kill creatures that fail saving throws; its Aura of Annihilation deals necrotic damage. It takes damage from sunlight and is associated with the demon prince Orcus.

UNRESOLVED THREAT: The Bodak remains in underground storage. Its presence has not been acknowledged by administration. It has not been removed.

Deeper Systems — Unknown Extent

Voss's original architectural diagrams (partial, held in the restricted archive) show underground chambers and sealed vaults that do not correspond to any known current facility documentation. Some appear to have been sealed before the academy's first students arrived. Their contents are unknown.

The underground infrastructure extends further than is publicly acknowledged. What lies in the lowest levels has not been mapped by any surviving document available for review.
