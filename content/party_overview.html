DM Only — Party Notes

  • Two Ranger archers at identical level creates potential tension — watch for duplication fatigue mid-campaign.
  • No healing beyond potions (2/student/year from [[academy_provisions|The Prov]]). The party is one bad session away from desperate. Keep [[academy_credits|black market]] available.
  • Caelum's social dominance means non-combat encounters often resolve quickly — seed complications that require multiple party members or cannot be talked through.
  • BloodRaven holds the [[sunken_fang_spear|Sunken Fang Spear]] — the party's most plot-significant item. Its origin in the [[jungle_site|jungle]] connects to [[ellery_voss|Ellery Voss]] and the FAIL Chamber mystery.
  • Gunnar's lockpick is a persistent "we could break into anything" tool. Either provide break-in opportunities or make clear which locks are magically sealed.
