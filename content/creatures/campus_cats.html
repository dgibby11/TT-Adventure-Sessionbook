Overview

There are somewhere between twenty and thirty cats on campus. No one feeds them officially. They are clearly fed. Faculty do not acknowledge them. Students name them, feed them constantly, and argue about which ones are which. Several cats have been on campus longer than any living student, verified by students comparing descriptions from upperclassmen to current animals and arriving at the same names. Faculty who have been here long enough to resolve the question have been asked. They change the subject.

Behavior

The cats go where they want. No door in the student-accessible areas of campus appears to stop them. They do not enter the [[underground_storage|underground storage]] system, which is one of the few campus areas without a documented cat sighting. They spend time near the [[old_library|Old Library]], the [[memorial_garden|Memorial Garden]], and the [[druids_grove|Druids grove]] at higher rates than their general distribution would predict. None of this has been formally studied.

DM Only

[[archdruid_sevra|Sevra]] knows which cats are familiars and whose. The list includes eight currently active bonds and three bonds where the bonded person is no longer on campus. One familiar's bond is with a student who graduated in Year 48 and has not returned. The familiar is still here, still present, which Sevra takes as information about that former student's continued existence and possibly their proximity. She has not tried to locate the student through the familiar because she decided some years ago that if the student wanted to be found they would have come back. She's been reconsidering this lately.

The cats do not enter the underground storage because the [[bodak|Bodak's]] presence registers to them as a predator they cannot outcompete. They know the death gaze kills. They have never needed to learn this explicitly. Students who have noticed that campus cats avoid certain areas entirely have not yet identified what those areas have in common.
