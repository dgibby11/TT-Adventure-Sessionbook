Overview

A presence in the Druids inner grove that is not fully corporeal and does not present itself in a form that can be easily described. It is detectable in the behavior of the plants — specific orientations, unusual growth patterns, movements that are not wind — and in a quality of attention that people who spend significant time in the grove begin to identify as distinct from the ambient environment. It responds to [[archdruid_sevra|Archdruid Sevra]]. It has opinions about certain students, expressed by redirecting their attention: plants that lean a specific direction, something underfoot that gives differently, the light in a corner of the grove that is not consistent with the sun's position.

History

It predates the Academy. It was here when the Druids Department chose this location, and it permitted the choice. Sevra has known this since approximately Year 15, when the spirit made its position on the tenancy clear enough for her to understand. She considers it a professional relationship and has managed it on those terms for several decades.

DM Only

The spirit is aware of what is happening underground and has been for a long time. It regards the [[ritual_floor|ritual floor's]] founding enchantment as something that was done to a location it considers its territory, and which it accepted on the understanding that the people who did it knew what they were doing. It is increasingly uncertain about that assessment. It does not communicate in language that players can access directly — Sevra translates, imperfectly, and not everything she translates is intended for students to receive. Players who want direct communication with the spirit need a Druidic or nature-based approach at DC 18 or higher, and what they receive will be accurate but not in a format that makes immediate sense. It communicates in what something was, not in what something is.
