Multiple dinosaur species inhabit the jungle field site, escalating in aggression. During the party's extraction they entered a blood frenzy — triggered by the [[sunken_fang_spear]] — and a Triceratops charged through the river in pursuit, breaching campus through the portal doorway before faculty killed it.

DM Only

Something in the jungle activates with the Sunken Fang Spear. Creatures can breach campus through unstable portals — a reusable threat vector.
