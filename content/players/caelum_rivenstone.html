Backstory

Caelum hails from a small village far from the bustling cities. Driven by wanderlust, he arrived in Neverwinter two years ago and quickly built a reputation performing on street corners and in taverns. He has since taken on occasional work for Lord Neverember — gathering information or swaying public opinion subtly. His life motto: "A song can soothe even the most savage beast, but always have an exit plan."

DM Notes

DC 15 save and +7 attack are the highest in the party — Hold Person and Sleep can trivialize single-target encounters. Cutting Words is easy to forget as a reaction; nudge the player when enemies roll well. The Actor feat means Caelum can impersonate voices — strong social infiltration hook. The Lord Neverember connection is from a prior campaign and may serve as backstory flavor or outside hooks. Dart +1 has Vex mastery — gives advantage on the next attack against the same target (good setup for Vicious Mockery + Hold Person combos).
