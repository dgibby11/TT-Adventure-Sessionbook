Metamagic

  • Careful Spell (1 SP) — Up to 5 creatures auto-succeed on a saving throw Ozzy forces. Safe AOE in a crowd.
  • Quickened Spell (2 SP) — Change casting time from 1 Action to Bonus Action. Lets Ozzy cast two spells in one turn (1 leveled + 1 cantrip, or quickened leveled + action leveled).
  • Subtle Spell (1 SP) — No verbal, somatic, or material components. Invisible casting.
  • Twinned Spell (1 SP) — Target a second creature with a single-target spell, OR increase effective spell level by 1.

Key Features & Traits

  • Innate Sorcery (2/Long Rest, BA) — 1 min: +1 to spell save DC; advantage on concentration saves; can apply 2 Metamagic options per spell.
  • Draconic Resilience — AC = 10 + DEX + CHA when unarmored; +10 HP max. Already factored in.
  • Elemental Affinity (Acid) — +5 (CHA) to one acid damage roll per spell cast.
  • Cold Damage trait — Resistance to cold damage; +5 to cold damage rolls.
  • Fury of the Small (4/Long Rest) — When hitting a creature larger than Ozzy, deal +4 extra damage. Once per turn.
  • Nimble Escape (every turn) — Disengage or Hide as a Bonus Action. Crucial for melee threat management.
  • Fey Ancestry — Advantage on saving throws to avoid or end the charmed condition.
  • Healer feat (Action + healer's kit) — Restore 1d6 + 4 + creature's max HD HP. One use per creature per Long Rest. Can stabilize dying = regain 1 HP.
