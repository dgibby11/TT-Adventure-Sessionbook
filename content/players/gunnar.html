DM Notes

Hunter's Lore is highly useful at FAIL Academy — most creatures have unusual immunities worth flagging. Colossus Slayer rewards targeting already-wounded enemies; coordinate with BloodRaven's burst damage. Low WIS (−1) means poor saves vs. Wisdom effects and mediocre spell DC — Misty Step is the main utility spell. The Improviser's Satchel and lock pick suggest a roguish side. Three-Dragon Ante set may open social/gambling hooks with other students.
