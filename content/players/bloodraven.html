DM Notes

BloodRaven is the party's primary damage dealer and anchor. Improved Critical (19–20) + Savage Attacker (reroll all damage dice, keep higher) creates very high burst variance — especially with the Sunken Fang Spear (+9, 1d6+8). Track the Spear's 1 Charge separately from HP; it likely recharges on a long rest (confirm from item entry). Second Wind's Tactical Shift (move 15 ft after activation without opportunity attacks) is easy to forget — strong for repositioning. Action Surge + Spear charge thrown + Greatsword follow-up can be a fight-ending combo.

At 54 HP with STR +7 and CON +5 saves, BloodRaven tanks most physical punishment — enemies with charm/fear effects (WIS +1, CHA −1) are the main vulnerability.
