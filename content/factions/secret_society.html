In Play

A secretive student organization that performs rituals and preserves forbidden knowledge. The party witnessed ritualistic chanting, hidden academic doctrines, and magical manifestations at a clandestine gathering by the [[founders_statue|Founder's Statue]]. Some students seek to uncover the school's true nature; others are already indoctrinated.

Motivations: uncovering the school's true nature? Serving a hidden purpose? Central to [[ninth_thesis|The Ninth Thesis]].

Founding-Era Roots

The original founding documents of the Alumni Society were removed from the [[old_library|restricted archive]] during the academy's second century — after the founding generation was dead. What they contained, and what replaced them, is unknown. Ashcroft's descendants still hold seats on the board of governors.
