Overview

The Arcane Dueling Society is the Academy's oldest student organisation, predating the current [[academy_traditions|administrative structure]] by at least two headmasters. It hosts a weekly open sparring session (Tuesday evenings) and a formal competitive bracket at end of term. All combat is regulated: elemental damage only, non-lethal stoppage rules enforced, a student marshal on hand with a standard First Aid protocol.

There is an ongoing, loudly contested rivalry between fighters and wizards in the Society. [[prof_thalia_varn|Varn]] finds this rivalry useful.

Membership Tiers

Apprentice
New members; can spar at open sessions but not compete in ranked bouts
Duelist
Three attended sessions + passed a basic marshal's safety check; eligible for ranked bouts
Ranked
Placed in any formal bracket; tracked on the Ranking Board outside the Annexe
Captain
Elected per term from Ranked members; runs sessions, sets bracket schedule

Academy Credits Connection

The Society holds a small betting pool during the formal bracket — strictly in [[academy_credits|Academy Credits]], technically unofficial, widely tolerated. The Headmistress has not commented on it. [[silas_morne|Silas Morne]] has noted it has no effect on overall credit circulation, which he finds interesting.

DM Only

The current Captain, Reza Ashford, has an exceptional win record and is beginning to attract attention from outside the Academy. [[thornwick_consortium|Thornwick Consortium]] representatives have attended two end-of-term brackets "as guests of the Academy." Reza does not know this and should not for now.

The Society's basement practice room connects to a side passage that leads to the [[underground_storage|Food Court underground]] — the Society's founders used it to move equipment. It has since been walled off on the underground side. The wall is not reinforced.
