Overview

The Expedition Volunteers organise and execute optional field expeditions to locations beyond the standard Academy curriculum. These trips generate course credit, offer access to sites not included in formal class schedules, and, as all members quietly understand, carry genuine risk. Three members have died in field expeditions since the club's founding fourteen years ago. The club has never used fewer than two members per team on any overnight mission. They are proud of both facts.

Other students call them "the ones who will definitely die." The Expedition Volunteers have a better survival rate than the average field exercise cohort. They point this out when given the opportunity.

Recent Expeditions

  • [[nether_mountains|Nether Mountains]] Survey — Geological mapping, ruins documentation. One member returned with frostbite and an unexplained burn scar. No other incidents reported.
  • [[river_rauvin|River Rauvin]] Bank Survey — Flora sampling and aquatic creature census. Encountered [[wolves|a wolf pack]] on the eastern bank. Retreated successfully.
  • Old Farmstead (Site TQ-7) — Abandoned structure near the Academy's eastern perimeter. Mapped, documented, not revisited. Members who went have not said why.

What They Know

Expedition Volunteers informally share notes on what they find. Their combined field records, kept in a binder called "The Log" in Room 4-C, represent the Academy's most accurate current knowledge of the surrounding terrain — more up to date and more honest than official faculty field reports. Any PC who asks nicely will probably be shown The Log.

DM Only

The Expedition Volunteers have been to Site TQ-7 (the old farmstead) twice. After the second visit, two members requested transfer to the main campus. One left the Academy entirely. No documentation was filed. The remaining members know something happened and have collectively decided not to go back. They will not tell PCs what Site TQ-7 is without significant trust-building. They will, however, mark it on a map if asked to point out "places not to go."

Site TQ-7 is connected to the [[ninth_thesis|Ninth Thesis]] thread — feel free to develop it as a side dungeon.
