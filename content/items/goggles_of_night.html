Type
Wondrous Item, Uncommon (requires attunement)
Store Price
250 gp — gold only; sold at [[academy_provisions|The Provisions Office]] Special Acquisitions cabinet
Appearance
Wide-lensed goggles with smoked glass that appears completely opaque in daylight. In darkness, the lenses glow faintly violet — visible to others within 5 feet.

While wearing these dark lenses, you have darkvision to a range of 60 feet. If you already have darkvision, wearing the goggles increases its range by 60 feet.

Popular with Rangers who lack natural darkvision, and students planning anything after-hours. [[silas_morne|Silas Morne]] notes dryly that these are his most popular Special Acquisition and is not surprised by this at all.

DM Only

The faculty watch has occasionally proposed banning the sale of Goggles of Night to students, arguing they facilitate curfew violations. [[lenny_goldvein|Lenny Goldvein]] has declined each time on the grounds that demand would simply shift to the [[academy_credits|black market]] and the Academy would lose the revenue. He is correct. He is aware of the irony.

If any PC buys these, [[silas_morne|Silas]] will make a small notation in his private ledger. Not as an accusation — just as a data point.
