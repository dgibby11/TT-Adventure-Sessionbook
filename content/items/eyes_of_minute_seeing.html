Type
Wondrous Item, Uncommon (requires attunement)
Store Price
250 gp — gold only; sold at [[academy_provisions|The Provisions Office]] Special Acquisitions cabinet
Appearance
A pair of magnifying spectacles with polished brass frames and lenses that are not quite the right size for a human eye. They appear unremarkable until worn.

While wearing these spectacles, you can see much better than normal out to a range of 1 foot. You have advantage on Intelligence (Investigation) checks that rely on sight while searching an area or studying an object within that range.

These spectacles arrived at the [[academy_provisions|Provisions Office]] six weeks ago as part of a "Gravelstone Recovery" estate lot — the same supplier as the [[bag_of_tricks|Bag of Tricks]]. The provenance documents list them as "recovered academic property, origin unclassified."

DM Only

These were originally the property of a former Academy librarian, Professor Yenna Morith, who disappeared three years ago. Her belongings were sold off by the Academy eight months after her disappearance was formally reclassified as "voluntary withdrawal." The PCs may recognise this name from records in the [[old_library|Old Library]].
