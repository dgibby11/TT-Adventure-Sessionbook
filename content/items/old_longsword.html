Description

A longsword with no Academy stamp. The crossguard has dried blood on it that predates any recent cleaning. The leather handle wrap is old — the kind of worn-in old that comes from regular use over years, not from age on a shelf. It is functional. It is in the armory. It has been there for at least a decade, and [[prof_thalia_varn|Prof. Varn]] notes it in the annual inventory each year with no acquisition date in the ledger.

No one has claimed it. No one has asked about it. It occupies a space on the far rack that other equipment is sorted around without comment.

DM Only

This sword belonged to a student in Year 38 who went on an [[expedition_club|Expedition Club]] trip and did not come back. The trip is in the records; the fatality is noted in the internal files as "field incident, Year 38, one student, circumstances reviewed." The sword appeared in the armory three weeks after the trip returned. No one reported putting it there. Varn was not yet department head that year. The faculty member who was is no longer at the Academy.

The sword itself has no magical properties. It is evidence. Specifically, it is evidence that someone brought it back from the field and put it in the armory rather than reporting it, which implies either they didn't want to report it or they were instructed not to. The blood on the crossguard is not the student's.
