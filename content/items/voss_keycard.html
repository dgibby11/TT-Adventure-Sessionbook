Description

A worn metal plate approximately three inches by two, with a pattern of punched holes that constitute a credential — the kind of physical authentication used in founding-era institutional architecture, now largely replaced by ward-based systems. [[ellery_voss|Ellery Voss's]] initials are stamped on the reverse. The plate looks old; the holes are clean, which suggests regular use at some point that has since stopped.

The door it opens does not appear on any current campus floor plan. Students who have found the plate and tried to use it as a general access key have had no results. Students who have found both the plate and the door — in the correct section of the East wing — have not reported back through any channel that would confirm they did.

DM Only

The keycard opens the ward on the [[wizards_tower_top|Wizards Tower seventh floor]] stair and, separately, the sealed passage between the East Annex and the [[ritual_floor|ritual floor]]. Both functions use the same physical authentication — the card was designed as a master credential by [[aldric_voss|Aldric Voss]] for his own use and inherited by [[ellery_voss|Ellery Voss]] as part of his personal materials. She used it regularly until Year 52, when something she found on the seventh floor persuaded her to stop going there. She left the card in the Eastern Wing rather than destroying it, which either indicates she intended to return or that destroying it was not straightforward.

The East Annex passage is the more significant access point for current campaign purposes: it leads to the ninth compass site and connects the investigation of the [[founders_compass|nine-pointed compass]] to the [[ritual_floor|ritual floor]] directly.
