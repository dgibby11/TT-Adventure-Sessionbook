Type
Wondrous Item, Uncommon
Variant
Gray (pulls Small or smaller beasts, max CR 1/2)
Store Price
300 gp — gold only; sold at [[academy_provisions|The Provisions Office]] Special Acquisitions cabinet
Charges
Recharges 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. Maximum 3 creatures out at a time.
Appearance
A small grey drawstring pouch. Weighs half a pound regardless of contents. Slightly warm to the touch.

This ordinary-looking grey bag contains a wondrous assortment of small fuzzy objects. As an action, you can pull one of these objects from the bag and throw it up to 20 feet. When it lands, it transforms into a creature. You can have no more than three such creatures at a time. The creature is friendly to you and your companions, and it acts on your turn.

DM Only

[[silas_morne|Silas]] has no record of this item's previous owner — the provenance document from Gravelstone Recovery lists it as "anonymous donation." It was not anonymous. The original owner is a current student who needed money fast. Silas chose not to ask questions. If the PCs ask who sold it to the store, Silas will say he does not know, which is technically true. He will look uncomfortable saying it.
