Type
Potion, Common
Effect
Drink as a Bonus Action. Regain 2d4 + 2 hit points.
Store Price
50 Academy Credits or 50 gp — at [[academy_provisions|The Provisions Office]] only
Purchase Cap
2 per student per academic year (logged by name by [[silas_morne|Silas Morne]])
Appearance
A small vial of red liquid that glimmers when shaken. Faintly sweet-smelling. Standard-issue. Bears the FAIL Academy crest embossed on the stopper.

The standard Potion of Healing is the most purchased item at [[academy_provisions|The Prov]] and the most rationed. The annual cap of two per student exists, officially, to "encourage students to develop sustainable combat and recovery strategies." Unofficially, everyone knows the school purchases potions in bulk at a discount and marks them up, and the cap keeps demand from outpacing the supply contract.

A second Potion of Healing may be available on the [[academy_credits|black market]] at 100 gp each — no cap, gold only, ask around at the [[food_court|Food Court]] after hours.
