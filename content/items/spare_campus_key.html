Description

One of [[torvald_thatch|Torvald Thatch's]] secondary keys, distinguishable from his main key ring by a specific notch pattern on the bow. Standard campus-issue iron, worn smooth from forty-one years of use. Opens the maintenance gate in the north wall — the sealed gate that does not appear on any current campus map and that Torvald has been asked about by approximately no one in the last two decades.

Torvald hasn't mentioned the key is missing. Whether he noticed and chose not to report it, or whether the choice not to report it preceded noticing, is the kind of question that would require asking Torvald, and he would not answer it directly.

DM Only

The key also opens the sealed east entrance to the Rogues mine — an entrance that students don't know about, accessed from outside the campus perimeter rather than through the main mine door. This entrance is older than the department's tenancy of the mine and was part of the original campus groundskeeping infrastructure. Torvald knows it exists. He has used it twice, both times to check something below the east ridge that the Rogues students' activity in the main mine disturbed. He does not know that [[tavin_crestfall|Crestfall]] is aware of the east entrance's existence, which Crestfall discovered through his mine mapping and noted in the [[crestfall_journal|journal]].

The key is currently in the possession of someone who found it — precisely who is left to the DM. Options: a student who picked it up thinking it was dropped by accident; someone who took it deliberately; Crestfall, who recognized what it was from the journal's notation.
