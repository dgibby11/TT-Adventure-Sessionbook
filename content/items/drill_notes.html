Description

A set of instructor training notes in a leather document case on the top shelf of the armory. Written in a precise hand, dated across a span of three years in the mid-forties. The notes describe training methods that are not currently taught in the Fighters Department. Two of the methods described are illegal in Silverymoon. The notes do not mention Silverymoon. They do not mention why the methods are designed the way they are.

The training methods in question produce specific results that go beyond conventional weapons proficiency — the notes describe behavioral conditioning alongside physical training in a way that the Fighters curriculum does not combine. The results described are the results of producing soldiers, not graduates.

DM Only

The notes are from the department headship preceding [[prof_thalia_varn|Varn]] by three positions — a faculty member who left the Academy in Year 32 under circumstances the official record describes as "retirement" and that students of that era describe differently. The methods described were used on a specific cohort of students in Years 28–31. At least five graduates of that cohort are currently employed in positions that suggest an unusual post-graduation trajectory. The training produced capable people. What it produced those people for was not disclosed to them.

Varn read the notes once. She kept them because destroying them would remove evidence of what was done, and she believes the record matters more than her comfort. She has not reported them because she doesn't know who to report them to in a way that wouldn't bury the information again.
