Description

A nine-pointed compass approximately four inches across, made of an alloy that doesn't match any current metallurgical standard. Each of the nine points corresponds to a location — eight of which have been identified as founding-era ritual sites on the campus grounds. The ninth point does not match any location on any current campus map. The compass needle for the ninth direction points consistently toward the east side of the main complex, approximately thirty-five degrees below horizontal, which implies the location is underground.

The eight identified sites are distributed across the campus in a pattern that is geometrically precise when plotted. The ninth point completes the geometry. It is not on any map. It is not listed in any campus record. The compass insists it exists.

DM Only

The ninth site is beneath the East Annex — a sub-basement that was sealed during construction and does not appear in the campus plans held by the administration. The [[voss_keycard|Voss keycard]] gives access to the passage connecting the East Annex to the [[ritual_floor|ritual floor]], and the sub-basement is accessible from that passage. The ninth site is where the third founding component is — the component [[brennan_ashcroft|Ashcroft]] was supposed to have taken with him. He left it here instead. The [[secret_society|Secret Society]] has the compass but has not yet identified the ninth site's access point.
