Magic Item — Shield (requires attunement)
Rarity
Rare (cursed)
Found
On a deceased adventurer, wedged in a rock crack on the [[nether_mountains|Nether Mountains]] ridge — Perception DC 16 to spot
Condition
Battered but functional; recognizable as magical (Arcana check)

A sturdy shield with faint arcane traces. The enchantment draws ranged attacks toward the bearer — a cursed property that is not apparent from inspection alone. The full effect only becomes clear once attuned.

DM Only

Curse: While attuned to this shield, you are cursed. Until the curse is broken, you are a magnet for ranged attacks. Whenever a ranged weapon attack is made against a target within 10 feet of you, the attacker must make a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the attacker must instead target you with the attack.

Story hook: A coin in the deceased adventurer's pouch dates to the same year as one of the crossed-out expedition dates at the [[mountain_base|base camp]] fire ring. This is not a coincidence — the adventurer was on that expedition. The shield may be why they were separated from their group. It drew every orc arrow during a skirmish and they were overwhelmed covering the retreat. The rest of the group made it back. Only Brec the ferryman knows one person came back alone — he doesn't know about the shield.

Identification: Identify spell or Legend Lore will reveal the full properties including the curse. Attuning without identification triggers the curse immediately — the bearer will know something is wrong within the first combat encounter but the curse is only removable by Remove Curse or similar.
