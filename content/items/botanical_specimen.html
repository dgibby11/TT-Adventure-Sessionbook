Type
Living plant cutting; sealed container
Source
[[druids_grove|Druids Grove]]
Status
In student possession; plant was not supposed to leave the grove

Description

A cutting from one of the plants in the Druids inner grove, kept in a small sealed glass container by a student who removed it without clearance. The cutting is alive — visibly alive, more alive than a cutting in a sealed container with no soil and no light source should be, though neither of those facts seems to be limiting it. The plant is responding to something. The something is not in the container.

The container has been checked for magical residue. There is magical residue, but it's ambient — the kind of residue a plant carries when it has been growing in a magically active environment for a long time. The response the plant is exhibiting is not explained by the residue it carries.

DM Only

The plant is responding to the [[grove_spirit|grove spirit]], which has extended enough attention to notice the cutting was removed and is maintaining a minimal connection to it. This is not malicious — the spirit is simply attentive to things that originated in its space. The practical effect is that the cutting can serve as a directional indicator toward the grove from anywhere on campus: the plant will orient toward the Druids building when the spirit's attention is active, which it reliably is between sunset and dawn.

[[archdruid_sevra|Sevra]] knows the cutting was taken. She has not acted on this because the spirit told her not to — it has a use for the cutting being off-site, which she has accepted without fully understanding. She will not explain the cutting's behavior to the student who took it unless they ask her directly with enough context to make the question specific.
