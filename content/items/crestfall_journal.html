Description

[[tavin_crestfall|Tavin Crestfall's]] field journal, left in the deep shafts of the Rogues mine and subsequently unlocatable when he returned for it. The journal contains two distinct types of content: routine navigation notes — routes, timing charts, depth measurements, junction markers — and something else, which begins approximately a third of the way through and becomes more frequent toward the end.

The non-routine entries reference something called "the permanent tenant" three times. No description accompanies any of the three references. The last routine entry ends mid-sentence. The word it stops on is "beneath."

DM Only

The journal is in the deep shafts, in a chamber that Crestfall reached on his third return attempt and could not find on the fourth. The chamber is real. The journal is real. The reason he couldn't find the chamber on the fourth attempt is that the [[mine_crawlers|mine crawlers]] relocated it — they moved it as part of their continuous reorganization of objects in the shafts, which they have been doing for decades in response to the residual enchantment from the [[ritual_floor|ritual floor]]. The journal is currently in a section of the shafts that Crestfall has not mapped. It is accessible to players who reach that section.

The non-routine entries contain more information than the summary suggests. The permanent tenant is described obliquely — never directly — through what it does rather than what it looks like: passages that were clear the day before are not navigable the next; sounds that are wrong in a specific way at specific junctions; one entry that describes the sensation of being observed from a direction that has no observable passage. Crestfall is a precise observer. His indirection is not imprecision.
