Title
"The Dinosaur Breach"
Status
Complete — Institutional Failure

Premise: Students participated in a practical FAIL midterm overseen by [[ellery_voss]], presented as a simulated jungle retrieval exercise inside a magical examination chamber.

Reality: The [[fail_chamber|FAIL Examination Chamber]] was secretly functioning as a controlled teleportation gateway, sending students to a real [[jungle_site|remote field site]] hundreds of miles away. Students were stripped of standard weapons and forced to improvise.

Key Events

  • Navigated dense jungle terrain; avoided or confronted [[dinosaurs]].
  • Discovered a mysterious flooded pit/well that was not supposed to exist.
  • Retrieved the [[sunken_fang_spear]].
  • Triggered violent behavioral changes in nearby creatures; dinosaurs entered a blood frenzy during extraction.
  • A Triceratops charged through the river in pursuit and breached campus through the portal doorway.
  • Faculty killed the dinosaur after the exam facility was heavily damaged.

Artifacts Retrieved

[[sunken_fang_spear|Sunken Fang Spear]] — unknown primary function; appears to influence creature behavior.

DM Only — Canon Established

The academy runs real off-campus operations and uses students in live field exercises · [[teleportation_network|teleportation infrastructure]] is unstable · artifacts influence ecosystems unpredictably · portal breaches can occur during extraction · [[ellery_voss|Dr. Voss]] is unusually competent (or skilled at deception) · the PCs are now campus-wide known.

Reputation Shifts

Strongly associated with "the Dinosaur Incident"; campus-wide rumors; faculty opinions split between concern, suspicion, admiration, fear, and recruitment interest.
